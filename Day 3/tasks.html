ARKET and Massproductions bring practicality and playfulness into the home.

It was an easy “yes” when ARKET invited us to collaborate. We are not only admirers and customers of ARKET stores and their products, but also neighbours on the island of Södermalm in central Stockholm. That made the project easier to manage, but probably also meant that we have similar values in terms of culture, design, and outlook on what a product should embody. No wonder then that we proudly present these five products as a truly symbiotic collaboration between ARKET and Massproductions.

H&M jet ski suit

This winter, H&M Move unveils its most elevated ski collection yet — a confident blend of high-fashion aesthetics and functional performance, designed to support you however you move. Launching October 30th at hm.com/move and selected stores, with additional styles arriving December 2nd.
